# MARTIAN MADNESS
## Characters
* Psychoanalyst: You start knowing 3 players and how many distinct character types they have.
* Ventriliquist: Each night, a player learns a role that is in play.
* Prankster: Each night, learn a good player: their brain is scrambled.
* Biologist: Each night, choose any number of roles. If any are in play, you do not wake tomorrow night.
* Mover: Once per game, at night, choose 2 players: They learn if the other chosen player is a demon.
* Instructor: Once per game, at night, choose a player (not yourself): They learn 2 living players that are not a Minion.
* Broadcaster: Once per game, during the day,  you may privately provide the storyteller with some information for the evil team to learn.
* Tour Guide: If your nearest alive neighbour is an Outsider, they learn you are the Tour Guide.
* Operator: If you learned something true tonight, you cannot die the next day or the subsequent night.
* Conspirator: If you are mad you are not the Conspirator, you cannot learn false information and you cannot be made brain scrambed.
* Zoo Keeper: If the Demon kills you, you are woken at night to choose a player: If they are not a Townsfolk, they die.
* Philanthropist: The 1st time you nominate a player, they cannot die today or tonight. That player learns you are the Philanthropist at night.
* Splicer: You have two (on-script) Townsfolk abilities. They may be in or out of play. You don't know which abilities you have.
* Test Subject: Once per game, when a player would learn information due to another player's ability, another player learns that information instead.
* Arms Dealer: You might not die durring the day. Evil players cannot die by execution unless all living good players vote.
* Deaf: You cannot learn information due to other character's abilities.
* Incubator: If you die, all good players are Brain Scrambled tonight.
* Stargazer: You start knowing any players who are an Outisider. Outsiders have a Townsfolk ability and they think they are that Townsfolk. [+1 or -1 Outsider]
* Brain Scrambler: Each night, chose 2 players and a role. Tonight, the first chosen player believes the second player has role selected and may recieve arbitrary role related information as if they were.
* Bio Weapon: Each night, chose any number of living players, they learn you are the Bio Weapon. Good can't win while you live. After good players successfully learn your role twice, your team wins on the following dusk.
* Expendable: Each night*, before dawn, choose to live or die. You cannot die by execution. Your ability functions even while dead.
* Dreadnaut: Each night*, choose a player: they die. The first time you die, you don't. Good players that nominate you are Brain Scrambled.
* Hive: Each night*, all evil players wake to strategize. They must collectively decide on a player to kill. The first time a Minion is nominated, they become an evil Hive at night.
* Corruptor: Each night*, choose a player: they die. If you would kill a living Townsfolk, that player to become an evil Corruptor and you die. Demons cannot vote while dead. [+2 Outsiders]
* Devil's Machine: Each night*, choose a player: they die. If you die, the storyteller gains your ability and the game continues. If the storyteller is executed with your ability, good wins, even if you are dead. A good player starts knowing the Devil's Machine is in play.
* Bully: Each night, choose a player: they learn something that is false.
* Mason: Each night, choose a player: they have no ability today, but cannot die this night.
* Hypnotist: Each night, choose a player, they are mad that you share their allignment. If they break madness, they might die.
* Mask Maker: Once per game, at night, you may choose a dead player: They are revived and you become their allignment. You don't know your allignment.
* Doll Collector: When a player of your allignment learns information due to another player's ability at night, you might also learn this information.
* Illusionist: A player who oposses your allignment is brain scrambled.
* Pilot: Start knowing a player who oposses your allignment. If you are most responsible for their death, something bad happens. You don't know your allignment.
* T H E    F R O G: You are the frog. You are amphibious. You may not talk. You may jump and croak
* Mind Distortion: This script contains roles that can inflict "Brain Scrambled." Brain scrambled players might incorrectly learn which characters or roles used their ability to interact with them. Players cannot falsely learn they have changed alignments or characters.
